Bettas are more than just a pretty fish

Betta Fish Are Living Creatures

 For several decades, Betta fish have been considered to be very easy starter pets to maintain. And while there are certainly more difficult fish out there to care for, Betta's are still living creatures that have needs as well.
 These poor fish are primarily kepy by breeders who horribly mistreat them. When these fish are ready to be transported to stores, they are placed in little baggies, without any food, for days until they have reached their destination. Many of these fish will die on the journey, and those who survive are rewarded by being placed on shelves until someone finally comes along to take them away.
 Unfortunately, that's not quite a saving grace for these fish. Often times, these fish are purchased by first time buyers who know very little about caring for them. They are denied any reasonable accomadations in their new home that they require to be happy. This often leads to many of them living a very depressed life, only waiting for their date of expiration.

If you own a betta, make sure you know they are getting the best possible home
